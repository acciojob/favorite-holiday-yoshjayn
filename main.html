Kyoto, Japan


Kyoto, the cultural heart of Japan, is famous for its stunning temples, traditional wooden houses, and breathtaking cherry blossoms. It is a perfect blend of ancient history and modern charm.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Fushimi Inari Shrine - Famous for its thousands of iconic red torii gates.
  2. Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) - A stunning Zen temple covered in gold leaf.
  3. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove - A mesmerizing forest of towering bamboo stalks.
  4. Kiyomizu-dera - A historic temple with a panoramic view of Kyoto.
  5. Nijo Castle - A well-preserved castle with beautiful gardens and intricate interiors.
